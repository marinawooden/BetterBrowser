BetterBrowser

A SQLite GUI.

Download

For MacOS: BetterBrowser-darwin-x64.dmg

For Windows: BetterBrowser-1.0.0 Setup.exe

What is this?

BetterBrowser is a SQLite GUI. Features include creating databases from sql files, importing csv, and pretty much anything you can do with SQLite, just with a visual interface.
